Welcome

“Empowering the Nation Through Discovery and Innovation: The National Science Foundation Strategic Plan for Fiscal Years (FY) 2011-2016” sets the Foundation’s direction for the next five years. The vision focuses it’s goals to better integrate them with the concepts of research and learning, and more closely align with NSF’s review criteria of intellectual merit and broader impacts.

This prototype represents Star Metrics’ analytic approach to viewing the data to better understand the broader implication and impact of NSF funding. Star Metrics demonstrates portfolio views that allow NSF to better compare its performance relevance in science and technology investments.


Recent News

STAR METRICS National Jobs Report: The STAR METRICS program is the first large-scale effort to document the results of federal S&T investments by directly linking inputs and outputs, using administrative records of research institutions. A first look at a national review of the jobs produced by Star Metrics from federal research investments from NSF, NIH, DOE and EPA will be released in September 2011.
